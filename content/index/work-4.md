Connected beam-test measurements and detector simulation to a quantitative reconstruction model for non-uniform sensor response.

- Combined beam-test measurements, track reconstruction, and Geant4 simulation to estimate effective detected energy.
- Used spatial light-collection efficiency maps instead of assuming uniform detector response.
- Quantified how non-uniform response contributes to energy-resolution performance across a broad energy range.

## Evidence
- The June 2024 test-beam note explicitly combines measured light-detection efficiency maps with Geant4 energy-deposition distributions to compute effective detected energy event by event.
- Simulations from 1 GeV to 34 GeV were used to fit the energy-resolution curve; the reported constant term for the beam-test map was on the order of `0.63%`.
