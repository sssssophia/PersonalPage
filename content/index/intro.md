I work on extracting reliable signals and physical observables from noisy systems, correcting bias, and validating models when direct ground truth is limited or unavailable.

Across research and detector-development projects, that has meant building probabilistic models, calibrating measurement effects, and testing whether conclusions stay stable when assumptions change. The same pattern carries directly into industry work on noisy customer data, weak labels, operational monitoring, and decision systems where robustness matters more than clean textbook datasets.
